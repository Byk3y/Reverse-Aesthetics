## FAQs

1) Are my results going to look natural?  
Yes. Our philosophy prioritizes proportion, subtlety, and harmony. We tailor plans to enhance, not change, your features.

2) How do I know which treatment is right for me?  
Start with a consultation. We assess your goals, lifestyle, and medical history, then recommend a clear, step‑by‑step plan.

3) Do you offer non‑surgical alternatives?  
Absolutely—threads, injectables, energy‑based tightening, and skin programs often achieve meaningful results without surgery.

4) How long is recovery?  
It depends on the treatment. Many services have minimal downtime. We outline recovery and aftercare before we begin.

5) Are treatments safe?  
We use medical‑grade products, accredited techniques, and hospital‑standard safety protocols. Suitability is always assessed first.

6) Do you offer payment plans?  
Ask our team during consultation; options may be available for selected treatments and packages.

7) Do you treat men and women?  
Yes. We regularly treat a diverse range of patients and tailor plans to each person’s anatomy and goals.

8) Where are you located?  
Lagos (Oniru, Lekki). Abuja (by appointment). See our Contact page for details and hours.
